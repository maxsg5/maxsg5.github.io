Who are we

LET'S START AT THE BEGINNING

Over thirty years ago, Richard (Rick) Hulme and Daryl Evans took a cross-country motorcycle trip that would change their path for years to come.
They both spent their days honing their crafts. Daryl, the engineer and builder behind innovative designs. Rick, the artist, and creator of high-end finishes.
Together, they crafted a company that still thrives thirty-six years later.

Get to know our team

Craig McKay

CEO

Graduating from Framingham with honors in Industrial Arts, Daryl gained an apprenticeship with master craftsman Mario Genevese in South Natick, MA building custom furniture. Several years of intense training with Mario led to employment with fine furniture maker Richard Monaco. Daryl works with our creative team on the build components of each job. He’s a measure twice, cut once kind of guy and ensures quality every step of the process.
