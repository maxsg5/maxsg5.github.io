About us

Founded by a meticulous woodworker with a passion for design, Ptarmigan is a small, bespoke studio offering clients a creative and practical, but ultimately personal, design-build service. We work closely with clients to design and build beautifully crafted built-ins, kitchens, custom cabinetry, and furniture that are a pleasure to come home to.
