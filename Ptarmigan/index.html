About us

Founded by a meticulous woodworker with a passion for design, Ptarmigan is a small, bespoke studio offering clients a creative and practical, but ultimately personal, design-build service. We work closely with clients to design and build beautifully crafted built-ins, kitchens, custom cabinetry, and furniture that are a pleasure to come home to.

Our Process

STEP 1

BRAINSTORM

You have a concept for a custom piece. We have a uniquely skilled design team to develop your idea and bring it to light.

STEP 2

DESIGN

Your vision is transformed into precise, detailed mock-ups and refined scaled shop drawings. We’re ready for our team to build.

STEP 3

DETAILS

Leave the nitty-gritty to us. If you’re working on-site, we’ll be there to field measure, install and make sure it is perfect.

STEP4

MASTERPIECE

Sit back and watch your vision unfold. Enjoy the praise.

Not convinced? Here's what we can offer.

PERSONALIZED SERVICE
Choose Ptarmigan for hassle-free installation and perfect fit, unlike Ikea. Our expert team handles everything, from precise measurements to seamless coordination, without any hidden fees.
SUSTAINABILITY
Sustainability isn't just a buzzword for us; it's a practice. We source materials responsibly and build to last, contrary to the disposable culture often seen with mass-produced furniture.
CUSTOMIZATION
Ptarmigan excels in customization. We tailor every project to fit your unique space and style, while with Ikea, customization is limited and often comes with a complex set of instructions.
INVESTMENT IN YOUR HOME
Investing in Ptarmigan's custom woodwork means investing in the beauty and value of your home, something that flat-packed, build-it-yourself options can't offer.
